Some of my projects

PrepMe was my final project for Grand Circus. I worked with three other students to create an application using Angular. The idea behind PrepMe came from a team member who was having trouble keeping track of all the companies they were looking at potentially applying to.

PrepMe allows users to create a profile with basic information. Their name, status, location, salary, skills, education -- so on so forth. Once a user created their account, they were able to create a list of companies they were interested in keeping their eye on. Information such as job description, the product, what type of skills were required for that position, as well as a company picture.
